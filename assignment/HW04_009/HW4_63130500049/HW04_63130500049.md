## Part 4: Visualization with GGplot2
### 4.1) plot graph show relation between Price and Number of Pages
```
ComSci %>% ggplot(aes(x=Number_Of_Pages,y=Price))+geom_point(aes(color=ComSci$Price))
scat_plot+geom_smooth()
```
